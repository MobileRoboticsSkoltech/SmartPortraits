SmartPortraits

Depth Powered Handheld Smartphone Dataset of Human Portraits
for State Estimation, Reconstruction and Synthesis

  • DOWNLOAD

Our dataset provides smartphone data (video and IMU) precisely synchronized with depth captured on Azure Kinect DK sensor as the source of ground truth information for 3D reconstruction, rendering, and single-shot depth estimation tasks. Recordings were performed with blinking flash for possible usage in re-lighting tasks.

Real large-scale data

200 persons, 5 different poses per person, 4 semi-circle trials per pose

Data diversity

Variety of gender, ages and appearance (with complicated cases for rendering, i.e., glasses, beard)

Blinking flash during recording

For re-lightning modeling and usage in albedo and surface estimation
